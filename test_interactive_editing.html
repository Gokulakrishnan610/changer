How to Use Interactive Editing
1
Hover for Info

Move your mouse over any session to see detailed information, conflicts, and availability status.

2
Drag to Move

Drag sessions to different time slots. Valid drop zones will be highlighted, conflicts prevented automatically.

3
Click to Edit

Click on any session to open the quick edit interface with available teachers and rooms for the selected time.

4
Save Changes

Make your changes, validate them in real-time, and save to update the schedule instantly.
